### creating databases

- List available databases:
- `show databases; `
- The general command for creating a database:
- `CREATE DATABASE database_name; `
- A specific example:
- `CREATE DATABASE soap_store; `

---
### Dropping Data

- `DROP DATABASE testing_db;`

---
